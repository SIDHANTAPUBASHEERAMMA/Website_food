About


We offer an authentic taste of South India in a cozy and welcoming setting. Our menu features a delectable range of traditional dishes from the region, including dosas, idlis, vadas, and a variety of aromatic sambar , chutneys,...all different kinds of south indian veg and non-veg meals and wide range of dishes from both veg and non-veg.We have alot of variety sweets and staters . Using fresh, high-quality ingredients and time-honored recipes, we strive to deliver vibrant flavors and a true South Indian culinary experience. Whether you're a fan of spicy curries or prefer the subtle elegance of a well-crafted dosa, Spice Haven is your go-to destination for a genuine taste of South Indian cuisine.
